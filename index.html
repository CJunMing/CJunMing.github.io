Job : Tech Lead

My original main job was handling the editor part of the engine and was changed to a tech lead. My task includes working on the editor and other systems like animation, physics, and scripting logic.

One thing that I learned from this project was the importance of splitting work for management instead of even work distribution. The reason for dividing work was to allow the programmer to work on a specific problem so that when it was complete, it allowed another programmer to take over and continue the job if the original programmer had an external issues. My colleagues during this project may face problems such as family-related issues or do not know how to do them. I took the initiative to help them as much as I could to reduce their workload.
